I have been constantly thinking that now is the time to unlearn/learn and build. AI has changed how we think about building, especially green-field projects. While a lead a bunch of AI initiatives at work, I constantly get this feeling that I am myself not building something fresh. So I had this thought if there is room to build something where its very easy for people to build their own agents - lets say you say something on whatsapp and there is a new whatsapp group created where you can chat and get things done. Example: I want to keep researching about whats happening in FPL - transfers, injuries, form etc and keep updating my team. I, as a user of this agentic-platform - could spin up a new `agent` that helps me figure these things and take action if needed. Too generic and could get stuck in a lot of hassles. Maybe I am just thinking of this as a learning exercise and dont really see this taking off as a real business model
